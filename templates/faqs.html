MLU Calculator: FAQs



Are you storing any data? No. We don't collect any data; it's all anonymous.
Any files stored are deleted within 5 minutes.

How does this whole thing work exactly? If you upload an audio file, we use offline speech-to-text recognition software that converts your audio into text.
Then, we use morpheme analysis to return an MLU to you.

How does it distinguish between utterances? Please use a period followed by a space to separate utterances.
i.e. "thank you. Thank you. thank"
Notice how grammar and capitalization don't matter to the algorithm.

How can I make the MLU be as accurate as possible?According to ASHA's standards, there should be at least 100 utterances when collecting MLUs.
